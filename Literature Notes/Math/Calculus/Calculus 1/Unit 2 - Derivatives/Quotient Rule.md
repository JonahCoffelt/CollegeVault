# Quotient Rule
Bottom times the derivative of the top minus top times derivative of bottom, over bottom squared
$$f'(x)=\frac{d}{dx}[\frac{\mu(x)}{v(x)}]=\boxed{\frac{\mu'(x)v(x)-\mu(x)v'(x)}{[v(x)]^2}}$$
## Example
$$f(x)=\frac{x^2}{cos(x)}$$
$$f'(x)=\frac{2xcos(x)+x^2sin(x)}{cos^2(x)}$$
