# Simple Power Rule
$$\boxed{f(x)=x^n}$$$$f'(x)=nx^{n-1},n\neq 0$$
## Example 1:
$$f(x)=x^2$$$$f'(x)=2x^{2-1}=2x$$
## Example 2:
$$f(x)=x^3$$$$f'(x)=3x^2$$
## Example 3:
$$f(x)=x^{-2.571}$$$$f'(x)=-2.571x^{-3.571}$$

# Rewriting Method
$$\frac{d}{dx}(\frac{1}{x})=\frac{d}{dx}(x^{-1})=-x^{-2}=-\frac{1}{x^2}$$
# General Power Rule:
$$f(x)=\mu^n$$$$f'(x)=n\mu^{n-1}\cdot\mu',n\neq 0$$
